Shipping policy

Shipping Policy

Processing Time:

  • Orders are processed within 1-3 business days (excluding weekends and holidays).
  • You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order has shipped.

Shipping Methods & Delivery Time:


    Standard Shipping: 5-10 business days.
  • Expedited Shipping: 2-5 business days.
  • Overnight Shipping: 1-2 business days.

Shipping Costs:

  • Free standard shipping on orders over $50.
  • A flat rate applies to orders under $50.
  • Expedited and overnight shipping rates are calculated at checkout.

Return Policy

Eligibility for Returns:

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery.
  • Products must be unused, unworn, and in original packaging with tags attached.

Return Process:

  • ontact our customer service team at support@VintageStory.com to initiate a return.
  • You will receive a return authorization and shipping instructions.
  • Ship your item back using a trackable shipping method.
  • Once received and inspected, refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days.

Refunds & Exchanges:

  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.
  • Exchanges are subject to stock availability.
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable.

Damaged or Defective Items:

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery, and we will arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost. For any questions, feel free to reach out to us at support@VintageStory.com. Thank you for shopping with A Vintage Story!
